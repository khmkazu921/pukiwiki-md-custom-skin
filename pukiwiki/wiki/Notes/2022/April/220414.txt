    scan](https://www.electronicdesign.com/technologies/test-measurement/article/21200893/exploring-the-basics-of-ac-scan)
    
      - AC  
        alternating current, 交流
      - DC  
        direct current, 直流
      - FT  
        final test
      - DC scan  
        Vcc scan, stuck-at testing. Here we target if any node is
        permanently struck either at Logic '1' or Logic '0'.These kind
        of faults are targetted in DC-SCAN.
      - AC scan  
        at-speed scanm, The transition or the at-speed faults are those
        faults that we check if the transition at the input side is
        really getting reflected at the output side with-in a clock
        cycle. These kind of faults will be targetted in AC-SCAN.
